Play the Game: Monty Knows Version
In this original version of the Let's Make a Deal game, it is assumed that Monty Hall knows which door the car is behind and will not reveal the location of the car until after the contestant has had the opportunity to switch doors.

Play the Game: Monty Does Not Know Version
In this new version of the Let's Make a Deal game, it is assumed that Monty Hall does not know which door the car is behind and may accidentally reveal the location of the car before the contestant has had the opportunity to switch doors. If this happens, the game is forfeited or annulled and the contestant is asked to play the game again.
